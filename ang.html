Anglicisms (anglicismos) en inglés y español

¿Qué son los anglicismos? || What are Anglicisms?

Los anglicismos son palabras o expresiones que provienen del inglés y se incorporan al vocabulario de otros idiomas. Este fenómeno lingüístico ocurre debido a la influencia cultural, tecnológica o económica de los países de habla inglesa. Los anglicismos pueden adoptar la forma original o adaptarse a las reglas gramaticales y fonéticas del idioma receptor.

Anglicisms are words or expressions that originate from English and are incorporated into the vocabulary of other languages. This linguistic phenomenon occurs due to the cultural, technological, or economic influence of English-speaking countries. Anglicisms can either maintain their original form or adapt to the grammatical and phonetic rules of the receiving language.

The English word "email" has been adopted in Spanish as "correo electrónico." This is an example of an anglicism, where the English expression is used in another language, adapting to the linguistic norms of that language.
