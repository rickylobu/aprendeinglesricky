Present Tenses and Gerund || Tiempos en Presente y Gerundio

Present Tenses - Tiempos del Presente

Los tiempos presentes en inglés se utilizan para expresar acciones que suceden en este momento o verdades generales.

The present tenses in English are used to express actions happening right now or general truths.

Present Simple - Presente Simple

Used for habits and routines.

Example: I walk to school every day. - Voy a la escuela todos los días.

Present Continuous - Presente Continuo

Used for actions happening at the moment.

Example: She is studying for her exam. - Ella está estudiando para su examen.

Present Perfect - Presente Perfecto

Used for actions completed in the recent past.

Example: They have already finished their lunch. - Ya han terminado su almuerzo.

Gerund - Gerundio

The gerund is the -ing form of a verb and is used as a noun.

  • Function - Función: Used as the subject or object of a sentence.
    Example: Swimming (subject) is my favorite activity. - Nadar (sujeto) es mi actividad favorita.
  • After Prepositions - Después de preposiciones: Used after prepositions.
    Example: She is good at dancing. - Ella es buena bailando.
  • After Certain Verbs - Después de ciertos verbos: Used after certain verbs.
    Example: I enjoy reading novels. - Disfruto leyendo novelas.
