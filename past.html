Past Tenses and Sequences - Pasados y Secuencias

Los tiempos pasados en inglés se usan para hablar de acciones que sucedieron antes del momento presente o en un secuencia de eventos.

Past tenses in English are used to talk about actions that happened before the present moment or in a sequence of events.

Past Simple - Pasado Simple

Used for actions completed at a specific point in the past.

Example: I visited London last year. - Visité Londres el año pasado.

Past Continuous - Pasado Continuo

Used for actions that were in progress at a specific time in the past.

Example: They were studying when I called. - Estaban estudiando cuando llamé.

Past Perfect - Pasado Perfecto

Used for actions completed before another action in the past.

Example: She had already eaten when I arrived. - Ella ya había comido cuando llegué.

Sequences in the Past - Secuencias en el Pasado

When talking about a sequence of events in the past, use the appropriate past tense for each action.

Example: First, he woke up. Then, he had breakfast. Finally, he went to work. - Primero, se despertó. Luego, desayunó. Finalmente, fue al trabajo.
